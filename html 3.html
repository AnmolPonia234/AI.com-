WHAT CAN WE DO?

Airplanes and cars also run mostly on fossil fuels. To reduce your carbon footprint, use less energy at home, switch to a wind or solar-energy provider, skip a long-haul flight, and drive less.

To reduce your impact on the climate, buy local and seasonal food, eat more plant-based meals, use up what you have, and compost any leftovers.

Speak up! Appeal to world leaders, urge your city, your bank and your employer to take urgent action toward net-zero emissions.
